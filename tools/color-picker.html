Critical Engineering Disclaimer

Use for digital approximation only. Screen rendering differs significantly from physical media. Be aware of:

  • Color Space: These values assume standard sRGB. AdobeRGB or P3 monitors will render these values differently.
  • Print Mismatch: CMYK conversions here are mathematical approximations and do not account for ICC profiles, ink density, or paper stock.
  • Calibration: Uncalibrated monitors can have Delta-E errors > 5.0.
