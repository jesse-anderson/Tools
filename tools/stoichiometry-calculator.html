Reference calculation only - verify before use

This calculator produces stoichiometric estimates from the formulas and masses you enter. It does not account for reagent purity, hydration state, solvent effects, incomplete reactions, side reactions, catalyst requirements, thermodynamic feasibility, kinetics, safety hazards, waste disposal, or analytical measurement uncertainty.

Balanced equations are computed by null-space linear algebra and may not reflect the chemically preferred pathway for redox or multi-step reactions. Under-determined systems yield one valid balance out of infinitely many.
