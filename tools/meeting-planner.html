    What is this?

    • A privacy-respecting availability planner. No accounts, no tracking.
    • Create an event, share a link, and your friends mark when they're free.
    • The combined availability is rendered as a heatmap so the best slot is obvious.

    Privacy

    Meeting Planner is server-backed: event details and participant rows are sent over HTTPS to scheduler.jesse-anderson.net so share links work. We do not use accounts, analytics, or application cookies.

    Privacy details

      How to use

      • Sign in with your name. Password is optional and protects your row from being edited by someone else with the same name.
      • Click and drag across the grid to mark when you're free.
      • Hover any cell to see who's available; darker cells mean more people are free.
      • Share the link at the top with others. The admin link (saved separately when you created the event) lets you lock or edit settings.

      Long retention (up to 5 years)

      Are you sure?

      Keeping availability data for years is unusual. We'll keep this event's data 1825 days (about 5 years) from creation, then permanently delete it. Participants can still delete their own rows at any time.

      The default is 90 days, with up to 2 years available in the Advanced tab without this step.

      Are you sure?

      Your row will be deleted within 24 hours (usually immediately). Platform backups age out within 30 days. See Privacy.

      Lock event
